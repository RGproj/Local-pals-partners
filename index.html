LocalPals Partner Model

How we work with selected experience providers

LocalPals connects international guests staying in villas and apartments in Croatia with a curated network of trusted local partners. This document outlines how the partnership works, the applicable business models, and the onboarding process.

01

What We Do

Guest requests
We source and qualify booking requests from international guests staying in private accommodations across Croatia. Partners receive confirmed, vetted inquiries — not raw leads.
Communication
All guest communication is managed by LocalPals from first inquiry to booking confirmation. Partners are not required to handle inbound requests directly.
Distribution
Selected partners are featured in curated digital catalogs and printed guest guides placed inside villas and boutique accommodations. Placement is editorial, not paid.
Quality standards
We maintain a limited and actively managed partner network. Reliability, response quality and guest experience are monitored on an ongoing basis.
02

How the Model Works

01

Guest contacts LocalPals

Guest makes an inquiry via the LocalPals concierge channel — digital catalog, printed guide, or direct outreach.

02

LocalPals matches and qualifies

We select the appropriate partner based on availability, category, guest profile and location. The guest is not redirected to the partner at this stage.

03

Booking handled through LocalPals

LocalPals confirms availability, closes the booking, and manages payment per the agreed model. The booking flow is controlled by LocalPals.

04

Partner receives confirmed booking

Partner is notified with full details — guest count, date, time, location, special requirements.

05

Partner delivers the service

Partner executes the service. Payment is settled per the agreed model. Guest feedback is collected by LocalPals.

Note: LocalPals controls the booking flow end to end. Partners do not communicate pricing, availability or payment terms directly with guests unless explicitly agreed in advance.
03

Business Model

We operate with two models depending on service category and partner preference. The applicable model is confirmed during onboarding.

ModelNameHow it works
A Full Payment
  • Guest pays LocalPals in full
  • Partner receives agreed share after service confirmation
  • Commission deducted at settlement
B Deposit + Balance
  • Guest pays deposit to LocalPals
  • LocalPals retains agreed commission from deposit
  • Partner receives advance / deposit share
  • Remaining balance paid directly to partner at time of service

Commission Structure

20%Standard Rate

Standard Commission

Applies to all partners by default. Deducted at settlement per the agreed payment model.

10%Preferred Rate

Partner Benefit Rate

For partners who commit to offering a recurring benefit to LocalPals guests. Agreed and documented during onboarding. See Section 05.

04

Partner Requirements

  • Responsive and reliable communication — confirmation within agreed response windows
  • Consistent service quality aligned with the standard communicated during onboarding
  • Clear and stable pricing — no unannounced changes after booking confirmation
  • Ability to handle international guests, including non-Croatian speakers
  • Accurate availability management — no cancellations after confirmed bookings without exceptional cause
05

LocalPals Guest Benefits

Partners who commit to offering a recurring benefit to LocalPals guests qualify for the 10% preferred commission rate instead of the standard 20%. This is agreed in advance and documented as part of the partner agreement.

Examples
Small discount on standard rate · Complimentary upgrade · Added value item (welcome drink, extra time, additional service element) · Special menu addition
Conditions
Any benefit is agreed in advance with partner approval. Partners may opt out of this arrangement at any time with 30 days notice, reverting to the standard 20% rate.
06

Distribution

  • Digital catalogs — curated service guides distributed to guests prior to and during their stay
  • Printed guest guides — premium printed materials placed inside participating villas and boutique accommodations
  • Direct concierge recommendation — personal recommendation by the LocalPals team during active guest interactions
Feature placement is editorial, not paid. It reflects partner quality and fit with active guest demand.

1. General Agreement

By submitting this application, the Partner agrees to work with LocalPals under the terms described in this document — including the applicable commission structure, payment model, and service standards communicated during onboarding. LocalPals acts as an intermediary between guests and partners and does not bear liability for the Partner's service delivery.

2. Bookings & Commission

All bookings are handled through LocalPals. The Partner agrees to the commission rate selected in this application (20% standard or 10% preferred) and will not circumvent the LocalPals booking flow by arranging direct commercial transactions with LocalPals guests outside of confirmed bookings.

3. Data Processing & GDPR

LocalPals (Bottom Line Advisory d.o.o.) processes Partner data in accordance with EU data protection law (GDPR — Regulation (EU) 2016/679). Data submitted via this form will be stored in the LocalPals partner database and used for partner relationship management, booking coordination, and communications. The Partner may request access, correction or deletion of their data at any time by contacting LocalPals at [email protected].

4. Communications & Catalog Inclusion

The Partner consents to receiving operational communications from LocalPals (email, WhatsApp) and to their business details and visual content being included in LocalPals digital and printed guest materials. Both consents may be withdrawn at any time in writing. Governing law: Republic of Croatia.
